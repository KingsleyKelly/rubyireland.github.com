Ruby/Rails meetup in Cork

There is a small Ruby Ireland group which meets in Cork on the last Wednesday of every month. This has been running since January 2010 and provides a informal setting for Ruby developers to meet

  • Location: Clarion Hotel, Lapps Quay, Cork city
  • When: from about 7:30pm on the last Wednesday of every month
  • What: Informal discussions and demos on anything Ruby- or Rails-related, or in the
    • wider context of startups and tech news.
  • Who: Anyone interested in Ruby or Rails development. If you’re wondering what Rails is about we’ll be happy to demo an app, walk through some code and help you get started on your own.

    • We meet at the top of these stairs... in the comfy couches and tables ;-)

    Contact: Jamie Lawrence (087 8035183)
